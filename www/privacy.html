Privacy Policy

We use the least amount of your data and guranteed security and encryption of your data. We use webrtc for establishing realtime connections and use socket.io for chat module. This means none of your chats are saved in our database, everything is realtime

If you record a meeting, the recording of the meeting is temporarily stored on Your browser as a blob file and when you stop recording, the file will be downloaded as a .webm file to your PC/Mobile. Your audio/video isn't saved anywhere and the functioning of our platform is clearly transparent. We, at Doge Meet don't use any of your private information as, our moto is to make a clean, spam-free and hassle free platform.
